Froyo Recipe

Description

This two ingredient mango froyo is healthy and easy to make!

This recipe is NOT an original recipe and is simply repeating information from this website.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup frozen mango

Instructions

  1. Place the Greek yogurt and mango in a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Serve immediately or freeze for 10-15 minutes before enjoying.
